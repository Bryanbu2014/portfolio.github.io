About Me

I'm a Malaysian student at the Deggendorf Institute of Technology where I'm working towards a Bachelor's degree in Artificial Intelligence. My passion lies in the field of Natural Language Processing, inspired by the rise of large language model (LLM). Throughout my academic journey, I find myself fascinated by the intricate ways in which LLMs can interpret, generate, and transform human language. Amazing, isn't it? This fascination has led me to seek out various projects related to the field.

I have developed skill sets in programming, especially in Python, along with data analysis, machine learning, and computer vision, all of which reinforce my capability to contribute to the field of AI. My experience includes undertaking various projects where I have applied these skills in practical settings, demonstrating my ability to innovate and create effective solutions.

Just a brief section about my hobby to get to know more about me. Besides studies, I really enjoy music, especially playing the piano. For me, it's a fun way to relax and it also helps me to be more creative and focused, which I believe is great for everything I do, including my studies and projects.

Thank you for taking the time to learn about me until this far. I'm always open to discussing innovative ideas, collaborative opportunities, or simply connecting with like-minded individuals!
